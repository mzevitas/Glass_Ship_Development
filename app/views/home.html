About Us


Glass Ship Development is a mobile application and web solutions crew that creates long-lasting digital platforms.

We found our roots in the heart of Atlanta, with a passion for creating iOS applications. Meeting with local business owners, we have created multiple mobile and web solutions which show our capabilities of creating modern, responsive, and long-lasting products for our customers. We call ourselves Glass Ship because we believe in communicating with clarity.
